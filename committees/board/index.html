Try my best to do always!

The board is responsible for coordinating the chapter's operations, as well as the strategic and long-term work of the chapter.
The operational work of the chapter shall be managed by the different committees and trustees, although the board shall act as a support if these should need it, and give guidelines for the operational work.
The primary tasks of the board are:

  • To take care of the chapters ongoing operational matters.
  • To administer budgeted means.
  • To coordinate the activities of the committees.
  • To summon and arrange the chapter meeting.
  • To work out and propose an operational plan and budget for the coming year to be approved by the chapter meeting.
  • To work strategically towards a vision, to develop the chapter's tong-term activities.

The board normally hold board meetings once per month, where each member report on their doings, and issues that do not need to be decided at a chapter meeting are brought up.

PM for the Board

Chapter President

The chapter's president is like the chapter's goalie.
The other electees deal with the day to day operations and events. As chapter president your job is to make sure that everyone else can keep doing their thing without issue, and at the same time solving any problems that pop up along the way.
There are few people in their early twenties that get the opportunity to be responsible for an organisation with a yearly turnover around 2MSEK with hundreds of members.
As chapter president you get the opportunity to take charge, with the support of others as you learn the ropes.

  • You represent the students towards THS central as well as KTH's EECS school.
  • You lead the the chapter's board and work with every part of the organisation and all the fantastic people that are engaged in the chapter.
  • As president your main task is to solve problems. They can be anything from keeping Kistan 2.0 accessible to students during exam periods to diplomatically presenting the chapter's opinion to KTH.
  • Together with the treasurer you are the chapter's authorized signatory and will be handling a lot of different agreements and contracts. You are also jointly responsible for the chapter's serving license.
Time: 999h/month Responsibility: all of it
